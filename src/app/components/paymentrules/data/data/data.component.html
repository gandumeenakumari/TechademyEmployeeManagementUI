Payment Rules Management

Policy

Techademy employees will have a fixed compensation stack i.e.,the component name and the framework of the
compensation stack is pre-defined.The amount per aomponent can vary from person to person depending on one's total compensation.

The diffrent components of the Total compensation is mentioned below in detail

Basic Slary

The Basic salary would be as per competency and as per Techademy Guidelines from time to time.The amount will be paid monthly and is taxable.

House Rent Allowance(HRA)

An HRA component is paid to help Techademy Employees meet a part of full expenditure for accomodation.This amount will be paid
monthly and will be eligible for tax excemption as per income tax regulations of the corresponding year.

Insurance

The insurance amount is a premium paid towards Medicalinsurance Benifits.

Profident Fund

Provident Fund(PF) is a statutory contribution under the social security scheme.Apart from the regular PF contribution,
Techademy employees willing to contribute additionally may do so using the voluntary Provident Fund(VPF) process
